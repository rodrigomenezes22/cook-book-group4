Lebanese Kibbeh

timer

30 min

palette

Easy to make

share

share

These traditional fried Lebanese kibbeh balls are a delicious appetizer made with bulgur wheat and seasoned ground beef and onions filling!

Ingredients

Kibbeh Mixture For Filling For Frying
1 ½ cups bulgur wheat 2 tablespoons olive oil Vegetable or canola oil for deep frying
½ cup water 1 pound 95% lean ground beef
1 onion quartered 1 large onion diced
½ cup fresh basil and/or mint 1 tablespoon 7 Spice
¼ cup kamouneh spice ¼ cup pine nuts
1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon salt
1 pound 100% lean ground beef
2 teaspoons 7 Spice
